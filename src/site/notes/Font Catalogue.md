## **Viturdesh Proper**
This is Viturdesh in a readable, minimal, typed font. It's the way the language will be portrayed in almost every non-handwritten form. It provides consistency in many forms from signage to web forums, tablets, and official documents. It can be thought of as the equivalent to Arial, Times New Roman, or Calibri, as an incredibly standard font for ease of use and readability.
